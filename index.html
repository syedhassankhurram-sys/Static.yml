About SchoolsInfo

Your Trusted School Discovery Platform

Finding the right school for your child has always been a challenge. Visiting each school physically, collecting brochures, and asking around takes weeks. SchoolsInfo changes that — we bring every school's information to your fingertips.

Our platform lists schools across all major areas of Karachi with detailed information about facilities, intake capacity, curriculum type, sports offerings, and location. Parents can filter, search, and compare schools in seconds.

Built with HTML5, JavaScript, and the Google Maps Geolocation API, SchoolsInfo works seamlessly across all modern browsers — Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and more.
